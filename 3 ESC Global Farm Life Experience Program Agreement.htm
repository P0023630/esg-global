Farm Life Experience Program Agreement

Agreement Overview
By registering for the ESC Global Farm Life Experience Program, you confirm that you have carefully reviewed your details prior to submission and that you understand and agree to the terms outlined below. This agreement ensures a safe, transparent, and rewarding experience for all participants.

1. Program Participation

  • The Farm Life Experience Program is designed to immerse participants in real, day‑to‑day farm activities.
  • Tasks may include (but are not limited to) mushroom harvesting, truffle cultivation demonstrations, planting and maintaining greens, bamboo fencing, composting, and general farm maintenance.
  • The program follows a “work with the farm” approach, meaning daily activities depend on the farm’s needs at the time of your visit.

2. Contribution and Payment

  • Program contributions cover accommodation, daily farm‑to‑table meals, and participation in farm activities.
  • Payment must be made in full prior to arrival to confirm your reservation.
  • Optional add‑ons (e.g., private rooms, additional nights) are subject to extra charges and availability.

3. Accommodation and Meals

  • Standard accommodation is shared; single rooms may be requested at an additional fee, subject to availability.
  • All meals are freshly prepared from organic farm produce where possible, reflecting our principle:
    🌿 Fresh from the Farm, Straight to Your Health

 

 

 

 

4. Health, Safety, and Insurance

  • Participants are expected to maintain personal health and travel insurance that covers medical treatment and accidents.
  • ESC Global takes all reasonable measures to ensure a safe environment, but participants acknowledge that farm work may involve physical activity and outdoor conditions.
  • Please disclose any dietary restrictions, allergies, or health concerns prior to arrival.

5. Code of Conduct

  • Respect for the community, environment, and fellow participants is expected at all times.
  • Smoking, alcohol, drugs, or disruptive behaviour are strictly prohibited.
  • Participants are expected to actively contribute to the program schedule and follow instructions provided by ESC Global coordinators.

6. Cancellations and Refunds

  • Notice of cancellation must be provided in writing.
  • Refunds may be subject to deductions for third‑party costs already incurred (e.g., accommodation or transport arrangements).
  • ESC Global reserves the right to reschedule or modify the program in the event of unforeseen circumstances (e.g., extreme weather, safety issues).

7. Media and Privacy

  • ESC Global may take photos or videos during the program for educational and promotional purposes.
  • If you do not wish to appear in media, please notify us in writing prior to the program.

8. Acknowledgement

By submitting your registration, you confirm that:

  • You have reviewed your personal details for accuracy.
  • You understand the nature of the ESC Global Farm Life Experience Program.
  • You agree to abide by this agreement and participate in good faith.
